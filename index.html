Yoga app

Yoga is a hobby I like to try and squeeze in between my manual job and gym based training. I genuinely believe it helps me towards injury prevention and recovery from all the physical activity I perform in my day to day life. This is why I chose this as the topic for my very first Basics SheCodes Final Project. Yoga is an ancient mind-body practice, originating from India, uniting physical postures (asanas), breathwork (pranayama), and meditation in order to foster strength, flexibility, and relaxation, offering benefits such as reduced stress and anxiety, better sleep, improved heart health, enhanced focus, and relief from chronic pain, serving as a holistic tool for overall well-being.

Fitness app

Fitness, especially resistance training, is a huge part of my life which is why I chose this topic for my Basics- Add on Final Project. Resistance training, also called strength or weight training, involves working muscles against an external force (like weights, bands, or body weight) to build strength, endurance, and muscle mass. It's an effective way to improve bone density, metabolism, joint health, and reduce injury risk, making daily tasks easier and improving overall physical and mental well-being. Key methods include free weights, machines, bands, and bodyweight exercises, requiring consistent, progressive effort for muscle fibers to adapt and grow stronger.
